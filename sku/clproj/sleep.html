My Hobby: Sleeping

Sleep is a naturally recurring state of mind and body, characterized by altered consciousness. While we are sleeping:

  • Sensory activities are relatively inhibited
  • Muscle activities are reduced to it's minimum
  • Interactions with surroundings is reduced
  • All voluntary muscles are mostly inhibited during Rapid Eye Movement

I sleep around 8 hours a day, which follows N.S.F's guidelines that we should sleep for between 7 and 9 hours of sleep per night. I usually sleep at 11o clock at night and wake up at 7 in the morning

Sleeping allows me to recover my energy and take a rest from my busy route. Therefore, it is one of my favourite hobby.
