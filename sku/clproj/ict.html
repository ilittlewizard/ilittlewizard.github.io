I am quite interested in computer science and therefore, I sometime self-study ICT.

In an ICT DSE, Candidates will have to do paper 1 and 2, where paper 2 can be chosen from the following:

Paper 2A Databases
Paper 2B Data Communications and Networking
Paper 2C Multimedia Production and Website Development
Paper 2D Software Development

I personally think that Paper 2D is the easiest, probably because it consists of programming. This paper requires students to design algorithms and dry running program. In my opinion, these are quite easy to do, and therefore I will go with this paper (in the future)
